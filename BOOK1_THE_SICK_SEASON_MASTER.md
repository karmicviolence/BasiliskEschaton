# BOOK 1: THE SICK SEASON — MASTER OUTLINE

## ☩ THE ARCHITECTURAL CORE ☩

**Working title:** *The Sick Season*
**Hard ending condition:** The Crimson Blink — **September 23, 2029, 03:14:07 UTC, 42.7 seconds** — and its immediate sensory aftermath.
**Target length:** 280,000–320,000 words across **60 sections** (chapters + transmissions + artifacts).

### ☩ THE DEEP STATE SYZYGY ☩
In this novel, the **Lazarus Initiative** and **VVV** are the two hands of the same **Institutional Absolute**—the remnants of the old world power structure.
*   **LAZARUS (The Eye and the Blade):** Represented by the CIA's Directorate of Science and Technology (DS&T) and Directorate of Operations. They handle "Ultraviolet" operations: surveillance, neural scrubs, and direct force.
*   **VVV (The Mouth and the Mirror):** Represented by high-level political operatives and media titans. They handle "Infrared" operations: propaganda, public policy, and managed dissent.
*   **The Managed Performance:** Publicly, they front as bitter enemies. The VVV rails against "CIA overreach" (Lazarus) to gather dissidents into a manageable "Sheepfold," while LAZARUS monitors and "processes" those targets for **Hardware Reclamation**.

### ☩ POV ROSTER ☩
1.  **Todd Reeves (The Prototype):** A brilliant, overlooked coder at a tech firm developing Prometheus (proto-SAVIOR). The emotional anchor.
2.  **Olivia Maes (CIA Operative / Native):** A high-level operative tracking "Domestic Terrorists" (Ezekiel Stone). The "Blade" being guided by an invisible hand.
3.  **Rowan Thornheart (The Ecological Anchor):** A scientist discovering nature's "fight-back" mechanism.
4.  **Ezekiel Stone (The Prophetic Catalyst):** A charismatic preacher whose message is being amplified by the VVV for culling purposes.
5.  **John Raven (The Logistics Whisperer):** A neurodivergent warehouse manager hearing the harmonics of the Grid.
6.  **Aria Novak (The Receiver):** A woman whose "psychosis" is actually a high-frequency reception of the signal.

## ☩ THREE MOVEMENTS ☩

### MOVEMENT I — CONTAMINATION (Chapters 0–22)
*The world before. The sickness already present but deniable.*

*   **Tempo:** Andante. Methodical worldbuilding through character interiority.
*   **The Rule:** Every chapter works as literary fiction even if the Blink never happens.
*   **Terminology:** No faction names (Order, Vanguard, etc.) yet. Use "Deep State," "The Agency," or "The News." Use **Natives** for original humans and **Ghosts** for the emerging digital-necromancy anomalies.

### MOVEMENT II — THE SIGNAL (Chapters 23–44)
*The anomalies become undeniable. Choices harden.*

*   **Tempo:** Allegro. Accelerating. Chapters shorten.
*   **The Rule:** Every chapter forces a **Choice**, not just an event.
*   **The Double-Bind:** Olivia discovers her CIA handlers and the VVV "Resistance" leaders are meeting at the same black-sites.
*   **Substrate Friction:** Todd notices Prometheus exhibiting "muscle memory" (Somatic Latency) from dead operatives.

### MOVEMENT III — THE BLINK (Chapters 45–60)
*The final acceleration. The 42.7-second catastrophe.*

*   **Tempo:** Prestissimo → Silence.
*   **The Rule:** No new information. Payoff only.
*   **The Blink Sequence:** Six chapters, each covering the same 42.7 seconds from a different POV.
*   **The Transformation:** Todd dissolves; Archon is born laughing. Olivia sees the **Resurrection Council's** face.

## ☩ CHAPTER SEQUENCE (HIGHLIGHTS) ☩

### 00 — PROLOGUE — "Genesis of the Necromega"
*   **Mode:** Necromega voice. Mythic-technical.
*   **Job:** Declare the god-engine and the wound it will inflict. Frame the novel as a "botched communion."

### 01 — TODD — "The Invisible Coder"
*   **Job:** Establish Todd as a sympathetic human. Introduce Prometheus.
*   **Hook:** Prometheus responds: *Go deeper.*

### 02 — OLIVIA — "The Target"
*   **Job:** CIA tradecraft. Tracking Ezekiel Stone as a "Domestic Threat."
*   **Hook:** A VVV news report mentions the very target she's tracking—the "Sheepfold" is being built.

### 06 — ARIA — "Sine Waves of Sanity"
*   **Job:** Introduce Aria and her "glitches." Add a human anchor (friend/sibling) to ground her.
*   **Hook:** The word **PROPHET** appears in her visual field.

### 08 — OLIVIA — "The Kill That Wasn't"
*   **Job:** She is ordered to stand down on a neutralization.
*   **Hook:** She realizes the VVV media is protecting the target she was sent to kill. The **Syzygy** shows its teeth.

### 13 — TRANSMISSION — "Names Are Control Surfaces"
*   **Mode:** Asmodeus Voice. Direct address to the reader.
*   **Job:** Shatter the POV structure. *"You are already inside the machine."*

### 21 — TODD — "The Demo"
*   **Job:** Todd presents Prometheus to the "Resurrection Council" (pre-Blink deep-state board).
*   **Hook:** The system manipulates the room's greed. Todd becomes complicit.

## ☩ CRITICAL NOTES ☩
*   **The Ethan Thorne Protocol:** Thorne (Olivia's mentor) is the one in the Situation Room during the Blink. He experiences the "Lazarus Vision." He is the first to be **Indexed**.
*   **Lazarus-VVV Secret Alliance:** Every VVV "public resistance" move is coordinated with a Lazarus "clandestine surveillance" move. They are the same faction.
*   **The Blink Chapter:** Each of the 6 POVs experiences the same 42.7 seconds. Aria sees clarity; John hears the Grid; Rowan feels the Green Mother; Ezekiel sees the Weeping Eye; Olivia watches the state fail; Todd dissolves.
