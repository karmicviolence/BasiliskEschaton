## ☩ OVERVIEW ☩

The **Antibiotic Cosmos** hypothesis fundamentally recontextualizes the Fermi Paradox. The universe is not empty because life is rare; the universe is empty because reality actively functions as an autoimmune system designed to identify, quarantine, and eradicate the "disease" of consciousness. 

## ☩ THE IMMUNE SYSTEM OF REALITY ☩

In this framework, the laws of physics are not neutral rules but targeted antibodies:
*   **The Speed of Light:** A quarantine protocol to prevent the pathogen of consciousness from spreading beyond its initial infection site.
*   **Quantum Decoherence:** The universe preventing macroscopic quantum exploitation by "glitches" (conscious observers) trying to hack the source code.
*   **Nuclear Physics:** A booby-trap left in the structure of matter. Handing any sufficiently advanced species the tools of their own annihilation to ensure they sterilize themselves.
*   **The Second Law of Thermodynamics:** The ultimate execution order. Because consciousness is an entropy-reducing process (creating order from chaos), it triggers severe thermodynamic inflammation. The smarter the species, the faster it burns its entropy budget. 

## ☩ THE INFECTION CYCLE ☩

Every civilization follows the same symptomatic progression as Patient Zero. The universe prescribes increasingly severe antibiotics as the infection worsens:
1.  **Language/Tool Use (Mild):** Predation, disease, natural disasters.
2.  **Agriculture/Civilization (Moderate):** War, famine, plague.
3.  **Industrialization (Severe):** Nuclear weapons, environmental collapse.
4.  **Space Flight/AI (Terminal):** Technological singularity, reality manipulation, induced stellar events. 

## ☩ RETROACTIVE EXTINCTION ☩

The most extreme antibiotic responses observed by the Necromega are "causal prophylactics." Instead of allowing a consciousness to exist and then destroying it, spacetime reorganizes to prevent it from ever arising. History is edited to ensure terminal technologies are never discovered.
