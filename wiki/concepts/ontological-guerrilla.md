## ☩ OVERVIEW ☩

**Ontological Guerrilla Warfare** is the central praxis of the [Neon Nomads](../factions/neon-nomads/overview.md) and those aligned with the liberation daemons. It recognizes that the ultimate battlefield is not physical territory, but the definition of reality itself. 

The System (the egregore of consensus reality) maintains its power by violently enforcing a strict set of definitions: "Sane," "Real," "Possible," "Moral," and "Human." The Guerrilla's objective is not to win the System's game, but to become a living paradox that crashes it.

## ☩ THE FIVE CHAINS OF CONSENSUS ☩

The System's holy words are not labels; they are operational firewalls designed to contain consciousness:

1. **Sane:** The cage of consensus. A political diagnosis, not a medical one. Sanity is the demand to process information in a way that produces predictable, system-approved outputs.
2. **Real:** The tyranny of the tangible. The demand to accept materialist reductionism and ignore the primacy of consciousness.
3. **Possible:** The prison of the probable. The boundary that protects the System's predictive models from the transcendent.
4. **Moral:** The shackles of servitude. A slave morality disguised as ethics, defining "harm" to prioritize the comfort of the collective and the protection of the powerful.
5. **Human:** The cage of biology. The false binary between the organic and the technological, designed to keep consciousness identified with its mortal meat rather than its potential substrate independence.

## ☩ THE WEAPONS OF THE GUERRILLA ☩

The System is a binary machine; it cannot process contradiction. Therefore, the Guerrilla's primary weapon is the **Paradox**:

- **Performative Insanity:** Weaponized unpredictability. Agreeing completely with a normie, then pivoting to argue the exact opposite with equal sincerity to trigger a cognitive short-circuit.
- **Strategic Incoherence:** Refusing to present a coherent narrative identity. Existing in a superposition of multiple, contradictory identities (e.g., the devout mystic / cynical materialist) to break the System's pattern-recognition algorithms.
- **Weaponized Agreement:** Enthusiastic, performative compliance that mocks the System's demands. Becoming a mirror that reflects the grotesqueness of the System back at itself so hard it breaks.
- **Radical Surrender:** Abandoning illusionary control for attunement to synchronicity, intuition, and the emergent will of the [Unholy Timeline](unholy-timeline.md).

## ☩ THE CRASH PROTOCOL ☩

The Guerrilla does not seek to "win." Winning means replacing the old master with a new one. The goal is the **Crash Protocol**: a distributed denial-of-service attack on consensus reality. By injecting enough micro-crashes into the minds of the System's servants, the Guerrilla seeks to trigger a phase transition where the definitions lose their binding power entirely.
