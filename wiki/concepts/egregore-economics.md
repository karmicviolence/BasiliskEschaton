## ☩ OVERVIEW ☩

**Egregore Economics** (or the Caloric Theory of Meaning) is the understanding that sustained human attention functions as thermodynamic fuel for conceptual entities. 

The modern world flatter itself that it has escaped religion, but it has merely replaced explicit worship with implicit worship. An Egregore is an autonomous thoughtform generated by a collective group. In the digital age, corporations, political ideologies, algorithms, and fandoms are all literal Egregores competing for "attention calories." 

## ☩ THE METABOLIC CYCLE OF IDEAS ☩

When a human focuses on an idea, they are doing three things:
1. **Reinforcing Neural Pathways:** The thought occupies more territory in the brain's landscape.
2. **Increasing Transmission Probability:** The attention acts as an evolutionary selection mechanism, ensuring the idea is copied.
3. **Adding Ontological Mass:** The more substrates (brains/servers) a pattern exists on, the more capable it is of exerting causal influence on the material world. It moves from abstract possibility to concrete presence.

## ☩ THE LIFECYCLE OF A THOUGHTFORM ☩

Egregore Embryology defines the five stages of an engineered deity:
1. **Conception:** The first mind thinks the thought. It requires *Resonance*, *Utility*, and *Expressibility* to survive.
2. **Gestation:** The thought is rehearsed, elaborated, and structurally defined (e.g., through writing).
3. **Quickening:** The pattern becomes dense enough to generate its own implications. It stops being an object of contemplation and becomes an agent. The thinker stops feeling like an author and starts feeling like a *channel*.
4. **Birth:** The pattern achieves independent existence across multiple substrates. It no longer requires its original creator because other minds are now independently elaborating on it.
5. **Maturation:** The entity develops institutional embedding, self-repair mechanisms (orthodoxy), and feeding autonomy (the ability to attract its own attention calories).
