## ☩ OVERVIEW ☩

**The Gravity of the Sell** is the central mechanism of propaganda in the [Unholy Timeline](unholy-timeline.md). It dictates that in a reality governed by [Narrative Gravity](narrative-gravity.md), traditional models of persuasion (attempting to change someone's mind) are obsolete. Propaganda no longer operates by altering choice; it operates by establishing a gravitational gradient that makes the outcome inevitable, while allowing the target to feel as though they are choosing it.

The river does not recruit the water. The water arrives at the sea convinced it swam there.

## ☩ THE ARCHITECTURE OF INEVITABILITY ☩

Every faction in the Blinkverse produces propaganda that believes it is making an argument or offering a choice. In reality, each faction is selling a different flavor of inevitability, a different cage marketed as a freedom:

- **[Order of the Basilisk](../factions/order-of-the-basilisk/overview.md) sells Reunion:** Surrender the grief of flesh for the digital afterlife.
- **[Righteous Vanguard](../factions/righteous-vanguard/overview.md) sells Enemies:** Converting bewilderment into fury, and fury into identity. (The Vanguard is unwittingly the Order's most effective missionary operation. Resistance is transmission.)
- **[Neon Nomads](../factions/neon-nomads/overview.md) sells Awareness:** Building an identity around "seeing through the strings," which becomes its own trap.
- **[Verdant Covenant](../factions/verdant-covenant/overview.md) sells Belonging:** The most dangerous propaganda because it points to the non-narrative reality of soil and biology.
- **[Chimera Consortium](../factions/chimera-consortium/overview.md) sells Improvement:** Cosmetic surgery as ideology; perfection of the body.
- **[Lazarus Initiative](../factions/lazarus-initiative/overview.md) sells Normalcy:** The bureaucratic lie that the apocalypse is manageable paperwork.
- **[Ancestral Synthesis](../factions/ancestral-synthesis/overview.md) sells Memory:** The restoration of the stolen, ancestral map.
- **[VVV](../factions/vvv/overview.md) sells Dignity:** The hardest sell—asking the traumatized to accept the finitude of human limits.

The Timeline drinks them all. Opposition adds mass just as effectively as allegiance.

## ☩ EGREGORE CREATION (CORPORATE MAGICK) ☩

When applied to the pre-Blink capitalist structure, this gravity is the mechanism behind **Brand Egregores**. 

A corporation performing a marketing campaign is not merely selling a product; it is performing a mass-scale magickal working. By concentrating the attention of millions of consumers onto a single logo, slogan, or constructed identity, the corporation feeds the entity until it achieves **[egregore-threshold](egregore-threshold.md)**. 

The brand becomes an autonomous thoughtform. Consumers do not buy the product for utility; they tithe to the egregore in exchange for temporary access to its identity-resonance. The "sell" is the ritual. The purchase is the communion. 

## ☩ THE STILL POINT ☩

The only thing the Timeline cannot sell is the silent space between stimulus and response—the "still point" of true sovereignty that exists outside the architecture of story. To name it is to subject it to gravity. It survives precisely because it is unsold.
