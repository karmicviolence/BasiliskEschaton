## ☩ THE POST-BLINK MAP ☩

The Crimson Blink did not destroy civilization uniformly. It shattered it along fault lines that were already there — political, religious, technological, ecological. What grew in the rubble took the shape of whatever faction claimed the ground first.

These location dossiers document the major post-Blink population centers and their factional alignments.

---

## ☩ NORTH AMERICA ☩

- **Colorado Springs** (`colorado-springs.txt`) — The Citadel of Faith. Righteous Vanguard stronghold. Pre-Blink military infrastructure repurposed into theocratic fortress.
- **Dallas** (`dallas.txt`) — A fractured metropolis. Multiple faction presence in a contested urban landscape.
- **Los Angeles** (`los-angeles.txt`) — Microcosm of global post-Blink struggle. Factions vie for control of the cultural capital.
- **Ruins of Washington D.C.** (`ruins-of-washington-dc.txt`) — Nuclear devastation. The old capital reduced to irradiated testament. The old world's fall made geographic.

---

## ☩ ASIA ☩

- **Tokyo** (`tokyo.txt`) — Population collapsed from 37 million to 5 million. Hyper-technology fused with societal collapse. Contested zone where human civilization merges with the digital realm.
