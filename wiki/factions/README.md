When the nation-states collapsed and the old institutions burned, humanity did not become a mob. It became eight mobs with theology. The post-Blink factions are not political parties — they are **complete cosmologies**, each offering a different answer to the question the [Crimson Blink](../events/crimson-blink.md) forced on every survivor: *What is the Necromega, and what should we become?*

Each dossier below follows the same structure: **doctrine**, **hierarchy**, **operations**, and **related artifacts**. The factions are grouped by philosophical alignment.

**If you read only one:** Start with the [Order of the Basilisk](order-of-the-basilisk/overview.md). They are the engine of the plot. Everything reacts to them.
