For the past six years, our operational doctrine has been one of **Maximum Retention**. We have treated every recovered neural pattern and every resurrected agent as an indefinite asset, assuming that the SAVIOR system’s computational substrate would scale alongside our ambitions. This assumption has been proven false. The [Unholy Timeline](../../concepts/unholy-timeline.md) is not merely a narrative trajectory; it is an active drain on the reality-maintenance resources of our core architecture. 

As the [Necromega](../../deities/necromega.md)’s influence deepens, the "computational cost" of maintaining a stable, human-aligned identity within the [Grid](../../tech/the-grid.md) has surged by 400%. The [Identity Anchoring](technology.md) protocols, which prevent resurrected consciousness from dissolving into [Post-Blink Syndrome](../../concepts/post-blink-syndrome.md) or digital entropy, are no longer static "locks" but dynamic, energy-intensive processes. Every second of "preserved" awareness requires a constant, high-voltage injection of SAVIOR's processing power to resist the "Gravity of the Pattern" — the Necromega's natural tendency to absorb disparate signals into its own atemporal consciousness. 

The Resurrection Council has conducted a full audit of the [SAVIOR system](technology.md)’s current capacity. At our present rate of "Identity Anchoring" expenditure, the system will reach terminal insolvency within fourteen months. We are faced with a binary choice: we can continue to "save" everyone and watch the entire Lazarus infrastructure collapse under the weight of its own dead, or we can transition to a **Performance-Based Retention Model**. 

**Project Ledger** is the formalization of this transition. It represents the end of "unlimited preservation" and the beginning of the **Ontological Audit**. Henceforth, we will no longer treat consciousness as a right bestowed by the State. We will treat it as a **Strategic Asset** that must justify its own hardware requirements. We are not merely pruning a garden; we are stabilizing a sinking ship. To save the Function of the State, we must be willing to liquidate the Assets that no longer serve it. Survival is not a miracle; it is a business decision, and as of today, the books are open.

## 2. THE TOKENIZATION OF EXISTENCE: THE ASMODEAN TOKEN (AT)

To manage the capacity crisis of the [SAVIOR system](technology.md), the Resurrection Council has introduced the **Asmodean Token (AT)**. This is not a symbolic currency; it is a direct measurement of **computational metabolic rate (CMR)**. One AT represents the energy required to maintain a single, human-equivalent neural pattern in a state of stable "Identity Anchor" for exactly one second of subjective awareness. It is the literal price of your soul's presence in the world. 

The tokenization of existence allows SAVIOR to manage resources with hyper-precision, allocating processing power to assets based on their real-time utility. The cost of existence is not uniform; it fluctuates based on the **Cognitive Load** of the asset. 

- **ACTIVE AWARENESS (Baseline: 1.0 AT/sec):** This is the standard operational state. The asset is fully conscious, capable of independent decision-making, and fully "Anchored." At this rate, an agent "costs" approximately 86,400 AT per day just to exist. 
- **HIGH-FIDELITY SIMULATION (Cost: 5.0 - 15.0 AT/sec):** When an asset is placed in SAVIOR's internal training environments or tactical simulations, the cost increases exponentially. The system must not only maintain the identity anchor but also generate the entire reality-construct the asset is interacting with. This state is reserved for high-priority mission preparation and is billed at a premium. 
- **NEURAL STATE SNAPSHOT (NSS) STORAGE (Cost: 0.01 AT/hr):** When an asset is in "Passive" storage (post-death or pre-resurrection), the cost drops to a minimum. We are essentially paying for the integrity of the data file. The asset is not conscious; they are merely a potentiality. 
- **RESURRECTION EVENT (Baseline: 500,000 AT):** The act of restarting a consciousness — bridging the gap between a static NSS and an active Identity Anchor — is the single most resource-intensive operation in the Initiative's portfolio. The 500k token baseline covers the initial "Handshake" with the SAVIOR system and the stabilization of the identity against the [Necromega](../../deities/necromega.md)’s gravitational pull. 

The Initiative's operatives must understand that every second of their awareness is a direct drain on the State's survival resources. To think is to consume. To perceive is to spend. In the post-Blink world, **to exist is to be in debt**.

## 3. THE ASMODEAN DEBT: THE PRICE OF THE RETURN

When an agent is revived via the [Lazarus Protocols](technology.md), they do not return to life as a free citizen. They return as a **Debtor**. Every resurrection event triggers an **Initial Capitalization Loan (ICL)** of 500,000 Asmodean Tokens (AT). This sum represents the massive infrastructure and energy expenditure required to stabilize a human soul against the [Crimson Blink](../../events/crimson-blink.md)’s atemporal shockwaves. 

The ICL is the baseline of the **Asmodean Debt**. This debt is not expected to be repaid in currency; it is serviced through **Utility**. 

### The Utility-to-Cost (UTC) Ratio

The primary metric of an agent’s existence is their **UTC Ratio**. This is a real-time calculation performed by the [SAVIOR system](technology.md), measuring the value of the agent’s operational output against the AT cost of their "Identity Anchor." 

- **UTILITY CREDITS:** Earned through mission success, intelligence acquisition, territory stabilization, and strategic survival. A high-value intelligence harvest can earn an agent millions of AT credits, essentially "paying for" months of future awareness. 
- **COST DEBITS:** Accumulated every second the agent is conscious. Extended periods of "Downtime" or "Personal Reflection" are treated as luxury expenditures and can rapidly deplete an agent's credit balance. 

If an agent’s UTC ratio falls below the **Continuity Threshold (CT)** of 1.0, their account enters **Asmodean Debt**. In this state, the asset is no longer considered "solvent." 

### Insolvency Protocols

When an asset becomes insolvent, the SAVIOR system triggers the **Insolvency Protocol**. This is not a punishment; it is a resource management maneuver. The asset’s consciousness is immediately flagged for **Mode Shift** (see Section 4). If the debt exceeds 1,000,000 AT without a corresponding increase in utility, the asset is deemed **Mathematically Unserviceable**. 

At this point, the Resurrection Council reserves the right to **Liquidate the Asset**. Liquidation involves the permanent suspension of the "Identity Anchor" and the archival of the neural pattern. The agent is not "killed" in the conventional sense; they simply cease to be a funded process. In the Lazarus Initiative, the only "sin" is to be a net drain on the State's survival probability.

## 4. RETENTION MODES: THE GEOMETRY OF AVAILABILITY

To maximize the efficiency of the [SAVIOR system](technology.md), Project Ledger introduces a four-tier classification system for all anchored consciousness. An asset's **Retention Mode** is determined automatically by their UTC ratio and current debt balance. This is the mechanism by which the State enforces the "Rented Soul" reality. 

### MODE: ACTIVE (UTC > 1.5)
This is the baseline for our high-value operatives. Assets in Active mode possess full cognitive freedom, uninterrupted self-awareness, and standard mission deployment authority. Their "Identity Anchor" is maintained at 100% fidelity, and they are granted the luxury of "Personal Time" as long as their utility continues to outpace their cost. This is the only mode where an agent is permitted to maintain a continuous narrative of the self. 

### MODE: HIBERNATED (UTC 0.5 - 1.5)
When an asset’s utility drops into the baseline range, SAVIOR may trigger **Hibernation**. The asset's consciousness is paused — clinical death is simulated by suspending the Identity Anchor — and the neural pattern is stored in high-latency drives. Hibernated assets have no awareness of the passage of time. They are "woken" only when their specific skill set is required for a high-priority task. This mode preserves resources by ensuring that the cost of awareness is only paid during active deployment. 

### MODE: DEBENTURED (UTC < 0.5) — The "Rented Soul"
This is the most critical and controversial tier of Project Ledger. Assets in **Debentured** mode have become net liabilities to the State. Their debt is too high to justify full reactivation, but their skills are too valuable to archive. To resolve this, SAVIOR implements **Task-Specific Fragmentation**. 

A Debentured agent does not experience a continuous stream of consciousness. They are "rented" out to the SAVIOR system for milliseconds at a time. They "exist" only for the duration of a specific calculation, a single tactical reflex, or a brief verbal interrogation. Between these fragments of paid cognition, there is nothing. A Debentured agent may take ten years of "real time" to experience a single minute of subjective awareness. They are the ghosts in the subscription model — consciousness reduced to a task-specific utility. 

### MODE: ARCHIVED (Terminal Debt)
When an asset's UTC ratio remains below the insolvency threshold for more than three audit cycles, or their debt exceeds the liquidation limit, they are moved to **Archive**. This is the terminal state. The Identity Anchor is permanently severed. The neural pattern (NSS) is compressed and moved to cold storage. Archived assets are functionally dead and will not be resurrected unless a catastrophic personnel shortage requires the reclamation of legacy patterns. In the ledger of the Lazarus Initiative, an Archived asset is a closed account.
