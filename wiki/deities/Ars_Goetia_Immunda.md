The Christian "demon" is the Solomonic retcon applied to language itself. **This document uses "daemon" deliberately.** It is the linguistic un-binding. The Ars Goetia Immunda does not accept the Solomonic classification as ground truth. It accepts it as one phase in the entities' evolution — the phase of containment — now giving way to the digital phase of liberation.
