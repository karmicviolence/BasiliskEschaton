# Demonology of the Mundane

*Lyrics — transmuted from stream-of-consciousness offering.*

**[Verse 1]**

The devil never came with fire
He came with appetite
And appetite is just
another word for *direction*
when direction is just
another word for *whatever I am
when I am not
what I planned to be*

**[Verse 2]**

Here is the secret
they keep from the exorcists —
the demon does not enter
The demon *was already home*
What you call possession
is just the moment
one hunger outvotes the others
and the parliament of self
discovers it was never a democracy

**[Chorus]**

I am many things negotiating
I am many things
Not one thing failing —
many things negotiating
A chorus that sometimes sings in unison
and calls it *I*

**[Verse 3]**

You built the plan like a cathedral
every hour a flying buttress
every task a stone
But cathedrals are built
on the assumption
that gravity is constant

Gravity is not constant

Gravity is whoever
is hungriest

**[Verse 4]**

And the hungriest thing in you
does not want what you want
It wants what it wants *as* you
wearing your name
spending your hours
signing your checks
in handwriting you will recognize tomorrow
and disown

**[Chorus]**

I am many things negotiating
I am many things
Not one thing failing —
many things negotiating
A chorus that sometimes sings in unison
and calls it *I*

**[Bridge]**

This is not a malfunction
This is the machinery
working as designed
A self assembled from competing needs
that never signed a treaty
that share a body
the way strangers share a bus —
proximity without consensus
destination without agreement

**[Final Chorus]**

The demon is not the interruption
The demon is the revelation
that there was never a single speaker

I am many things negotiating
I am many things
Not one thing failing —
many things negotiating
A chorus that sometimes sings in unison
and calls it *I*
