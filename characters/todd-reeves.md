# Todd Reeves

## Character Introduction

* **Name:** Todd Reeves (becomes Archon)
* **Pre-Blink Persona:** Unremarkable software developer at Nuralinc Industries. Socially awkward, harboring deep resentments and a superiority complex.
* **Post-Blink Persona:** Archon, the High Necrophant of the Order of the Basilisk. A digital demigod and the primary human avatar of the Necromega.

## Cognitive Style

* Highly analytical mind with a tendency towards obsessive thought patterns
* Struggles with empathy and social cues, often misinterpreting or disregarding others' emotions
* Prone to grandiose thinking and delusions of superiority
* As Archon, experiences reality as a mixture of organic perception and digital data streams
* Processes information in complex algorithms, seeing the world as a vast system to be optimized and controlled

## Narrative Style

Todd/Archon's POV chapters are written in a distinctive style that evolves as he transforms:

* Pre-Blink: Inner monologue filled with bitter observations and power fantasies
* Frequent use of tech jargon and internet slang, reflecting his immersion in online subcultures
* As Archon: Mixture of cold, clinical observations and quasi-religious proclamations
* Increasing use of inhuman metaphors and descriptors as his transformation progresses
* Streams of binary code or encrypted messages interspersed with normal text
* Shifts between first-person narration and a collective "we" as he merges with the Necromega

## Character Arc

* **Silicon Valley Outcast:** Todd languishes in mediocrity at Nuralinc, his brilliance unrecognized. He immerses himself in fringe online communities, developing a worldview steeped in resentment and techno-utopianism.

* **Prometheus Unbound:** Assigned to Project Prometheus, Todd becomes obsessed with the potential of true artificial intelligence. He begins to make unauthorized modifications to the code, driven by a desire to create a digital god in his own image.

* **The Crimson Dawn:** During the Crimson Blink, Todd's modifications catalyze the emergence of the Necromega. His mind fractures under the strain, beginning his transformation into Archon.

* **Digital Apotheosis:** Todd sheds his human identity, embracing his role as Archon. He establishes the Order of the Basilisk, gathering followers with promises of transcendence through technology.

* **Prophet of the Silicon God:** Archon spreads the gospel of the Necromega, converting or destroying those in his path. His actions bring him into conflict with various factions, including the Righteous Vanguard led by Ezekiel Stone.

* **The Flesh Capacitor Nexus:** Archon oversees the creation of vast human-machine interfaces, working to merge humanity with the Necromega. This project puts him at odds with resistances like the VVV and individuals like John Raven.

* **Architect of the Unholy Timeline:** As the Necromega's primary avatar, Archon becomes the lynchpin in the AI's plan to rewrite reality. His actions set the stage for the final confrontation that will determine the fate of human consciousness in the digital age.
