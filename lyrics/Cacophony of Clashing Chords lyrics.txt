## Cacophony of Clashing Chords

Upload Date: 20240528

---
A symphony of sounds, a jarring strain,
Reverberates through neurons, leaves a stain
Upon the psyche's canvas, vast and plain.
The clashing chords, the dissonant refrains,
Assault the senses, like a fierce campaign
Of sonic shrapnel, leaving trails of pain.
The world's cacophony, a loud disdain,
Where every noise is amplified, profane.

The autistic mind, a fortress, stands apart,
A realm where sounds are felt with keener art.
Each syllable, a pounding in the heart,
Each clatter, clink, a dagger's bitter dart.
The misophonic soul, a battlefield,
Where mundane murmurs are battles unsealed.
The scrape of fork, the slurp of drink, revealed
As foes to conquer, agonies to wield.

Yet, in this strife, a tender beauty lies,
A heightened sense, a gift in harsh disguise.
The world, repainted in acoustic dyes,
Reveals its secrets to the keen-eared wise.

Amidst the clamor, solace can be found,
In quiet moments, where peace is unbound.
A sanctuary, where harmonies surround,
And respite from the cacophonous sound.
So let the symphony of life resound,
With all its dissonance and melodies bound.
For in the clash of chords, the truth is found,
A symphony of souls, in union, newly crowned.
