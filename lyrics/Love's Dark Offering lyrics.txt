## Love's Dark Offering

Upload Date: 20240414

---
Beneath the fluorescent sheen, where shelves convene,
I stand, a shadow draped in aisles of light,
Not here for bargains, no, a grander scene
Unfolds: a symphony of fear, my dark delight.
Each apple blushed, each melon's emerald gleam
Conceals the hunger gnawing at my soul,
Not bread nor wine sustains this twisted dream,
But murmurs, grateful words, that make me whole.

She smiles, unknowing, at the checkout lane,
Her purchases a mundane melody.
I linger, watch her leave, a predator's refrain,
Her every step stirs darkened fantasy.
In shadows' cloak, I trace her steps unseen,
Through crowded streets where light and darkness blend,
My heart, a drum, beats to a rhythm keen,
Each step she takes, draws closer to her end.

With gratitude, I find her door ajar,
A silent invitation, fate's own lure.
Through velvet shadows, under evening's star,
I step inside, my purpose dark and pure.
Bags stowed away, a hum upon her breath,
She fills the kitchen, bathed in sunlit grace.
Each jar a secret, waiting 'neath my stealth,
A map to whisper through her slumber's space.

Day's embers die, a hush descends the stair,
She bids farewell to light, a whispered vow.
I watch her form, a sculpture unaware,
As sleep's embrace prepares the silent bough.
The house breathes quiet, secrets held within,
Each room a chapter of her life untold.
In this hushed sanctum, my new life begins,
A story dark and desperate to unfold.

My endless thanks, I now impart,
This final act, a requiem I sing.
The night unfurls its maw, a chilling start,
A canvas painted crimson, love's dark offering.
