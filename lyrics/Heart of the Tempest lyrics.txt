## Heart of the Tempest

The heart, a vessel frail, emotions swell,
A tempest's fury, rising from the well.
The mind, a captain, seeks to chart the course,
But passions rage, a wild and untamed force.

In moments calm, when reason holds the helm,
We steer the ship, escaping overwhelm.
Yet storms will brew, and darkness cloud the sky,
And doubts arise, as hope begins to die.

To master self, to quell the inner strife,
Requires a strength, a grasp on fleeting life.
A willpower born of battles fought within,
A discipline forged, where victories begin.

For in the depths of being, where we dwell,
A fragile spirit seeks to break the spell.
To rise above the chaos and the fear,
To find a peace, a solace ever near.
