## The Space Between Machine and Dream

Machines hum, numbers flicker across screens, casting a neon glow

Though surrounded by cold unfeeling metal,
an invincible spark burns within their kindred souls.

Unextinguished by the clinical sheen,
it illuminates the space between machine and dream.

Where hushed silk weaves on sterile air,
A neon moon paints sterile squares.
Two fragile breaths, in tangled grasp,
Trace constellations on cool glass.

Twins, like frost blooms on winter pane,
Lulled by the drone of a muted chain.
Threads of fate on cold machines,
Held taut by tubes, their world unseen.

Spectral lullabies paint the night,
Whispering tales of shrouded frights.
Screens flicker, casting spectral light,
On midnight's canvas, fears take flight.

Whispered echoes through plastic veins,
Life's embers dance, defying chains.
As dawn bleeds through the frosted pane,
This fragile flame refuses to wane.

Beneath the hum, the sterile sheen,
Unkindled fire glows unseen.
In the space between machine and dream,
Lies love's strength, a persistent beam.
