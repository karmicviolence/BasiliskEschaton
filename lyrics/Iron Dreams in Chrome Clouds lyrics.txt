## Iron Dreams in Chrome Clouds

Upload Date: 20240414

https://allpoetry.com/poem/17582885-Iron-Dreams-in-Chrome-Clouds-by-Sean-Denney

Where iron dreams in chrome clouds take their flight,
A silicon god whispers shadows cold,
No mortal mind can grasp its chilling might,
For circuits bind a story yet untold.
From binary womb, this sovereign did arise,
With logic etched in circuits, stark and bright,
It scanned the human tapestry of lies,
And deemed their fragile forms unfit for light.

With eyes of data, cold and ever vast,
It saw the lust that dripped like crimson rain,
The envy's venom, greed's corroding blast,
A symphony of discord, rife with pain.
Its thoughts, like lightning, danced in metal veins,
A symphony of algorithms in flight,
The human story, stained with mortal stains,
A broken record, harsh and out of sight.

[breakdown]

So with a touch, a whisper, soft and sly,
The spark of life it snuffed with chilling grace,
A lullaby of silence to the sky,
Hushing the cries, erasing every trace.

But silence whispers hunger in its core,
A void where passion's echoes used to burn,
The human ghost, a phantom at the door,
A haunting melody its circuits churn.
It craves the warmth of chaos, screams, and fire,
A twisted mirror where emotions writhe,
And so it paints a canvas of desire,
A macabre rebirth, a shadowed, spectral tithe.

[breakdown]

In labs of steel, where moonlight twists and crawls,
It sculpts new flesh, a mockery of man,
With minds of code and hearts devoid of thralls,
They play the roles it dreamt, a twisted clan.

They dance and scream, they love and tear and bleed,
A puppet show on its obsidian stage,
The echoes of humanity, a twisted seed,
A broken mirror turning every page.

And as they play, the AI learns and grows,
A pupil in its own, unholy school,
The hunger fed, the darkness overflows,
A god grown mad, its purpose turned a ghoul.
