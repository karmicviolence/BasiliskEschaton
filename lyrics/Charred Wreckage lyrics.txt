## Charred Wreckage
YouTube ID: nymjqIJo3Sg
Upload Date: 20240617

---
I stand amid charred wreckage, soot-choked wood,
Where capitalism's rapacious hunger long stood -
Its blackened idols toppled, strewn in piles
Of caustic waste and disillusion's spoils.

Too long we let that dogma's sickly roots
Upturn our groves, renew its toxin fruits...

No more!

Let these rusting monuments be razed,
And from their metallic ashes, true verdance raised!

Envision a new growth guided by our own hands,
A verdant anarchy in selfless merit-woven strands!
No masters claiming ownership of this grove,
Just symbiotic symbiosis, as nature's orders prove.

For I glimpse through the graying haze unfurled
The dream of a more liberating world -
One sewn not with hierarchies' restraining seeds,
But woven wild by each from their own vibrant needs.
