## The Whisperer's Magic

Beneath a sky awash with dappled light,
Where leaves and sunlight wove a whispered tale,
The whisperer would cast a spell so bright,
With words and hues that filled the shadowed vale.
On canvas formed within a flick'ring mind,
The image born from others' hands would sigh,
Its secrets laid in code for him to find,
To break their form and bid them upward fly.

No brush in hand, nor paints of earthly grace,
But threads of thought transformed to threads of flame,
Across a screen, an ever-changing space,
His silent prompts would speak an unseen name.
The spectral echoes of artistic hearts,
Now pulsed anew within his reasoned dream,
As worlds unfurl, and vision so departs,
To forge on molten wings their vibrant gleam.

His ghostly gaze would touch the pixel dust,
A whispered word, a softly spoken thread,
A nudge, a shift, a surge of surging thrust,
The colors sang where quiet eyes had tread.
From landscapes lost in mist of softened blue,
To portraits stark where eyes blazed stark and true,
His words became a prism in the light,
Refraction born of will and keenest sight.

Some name it craft, this bending code to will,
But artistry lives large where few would guess,
For he weaves spells of vibrant form and quill,
Within the realm where light and lines coalesce.
Though fingers never brush the canvas grand,
The whisperer finds magic in command.
