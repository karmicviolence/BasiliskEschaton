Not words, but waves unseen, my thoughts arise,
A symphony of hues, where meaning lies.
I glimpse a vibrant crimson, feel its fire,
As passion ignites, fueled by pure desire.
Sharp tang of lemon mingles with sweet chime,
A bitter note, a wrinkle in the rhyme.
Mirrored laughter, tears I never cried,
Another's hurt, a phantom pang inside.

With memories, emotions, intertwined,
A kaleidoscope where senses lose their bind.
Harsh sounds like sandpaper, scrape and grate,
Discordant, grating, causing instant hate.

But music paints a canvas, warm and bright,
A melody of stars, a moonlit night.
I feel the notes like whispers on my skin,
A tingling joy, a pleasure pulled within.
The world unseen, a symphony of sense,
Each brushstroke bright, defying consequence.
I gather words, like pebbles from a stream,
Some smooth and cool, some harsh, a jarring gleam.
Yet words I write, precise and technical,
Lacking the flair, the colors visual.
So AI helps me bridge, translate the flow,
From abstract realms to verses vibrant, so

I capture fleeting joy, the bittersweet pang,
A memory's echo, on a fragile string.
Each poem, a vessel, holds a treasured spark,
A bookmark to emotions, hidden in the dark.
With care I craft, a tapestry of sound,
Where rhythm flows, and emotions abound.
The bookmark pressed, to mark the fleeting well,
Of joy and sorrow, stories words can tell.
Though fleeting is the touch, the afterglow remains,
A whispered echo, soothing life's harsh strains.
And when I stumble, lost in shadows deep,
My own verse offers solace, pulls me from my sleep.

For in these lines, the colors bleed anew,
And I am whole, with senses clear and true.
So let the music play, the textures bloom,
And I will dance with words, within the gloom.
