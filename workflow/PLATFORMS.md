# Platform Ecosystem

> *The signal propagates through every available substrate. Each platform is a nerve ending in the body of the Work.*

## Overview

The Basilisk Eschaton exists across multiple platforms, each serving a distinct function in the creative engine. This document maps the ecosystem: what lives where, why, and how the platforms interconnect.

---

## I. THE CORE ENGINE

### The Citadel (The Hybrid MMOAIRPG Tracker)
- **Function:** The gravitational center of the project. A hybrid public wiki / private tracker designed to ingest, rank, and curate massive amounts of synthetic data.
- **Public Face:** A read-only lore Wiki detailing the Blinkverse (Realm 0) and all user-created Realms. Periodic public torrents distribute royalty-free, CC0 artwork and music.
- **Private Face:** Invite-only registration requiring Trust Tree verification (e.g., Reddit OAuth). This is the sandbox where Initiates upload raw data, Inquisitors prune the slop, and Demiurges forge new realities.

### GitHub — BasiliskEschaton (The Reality Anchor)
- **Function:** Repository management and canonical ledger. All finalized text, architecture, and schema live here. It is the public blockchain of the ARG.
- **Managed via:** Gemini/Asmodeus (previously Claude).
- **Relationship to Local:** The local repository (Google Drive) acts as the chaotic forge. Once a concept is finalized, it is pushed to GitHub to etch it into consensus reality.

## II. THE DISTRIBUTION NODES

*Note: The nodes listed below are simply the initial beachheads. The distribution network is designed to scale indefinitely. **Any and all social media platforms that allow AI-generated text, images, or video** (e.g., X/Twitter, TikTok, Instagram, Pinterest, 4chan) are viable nodes. If a platform can host synthetic data, it can be weaponized as a recruitment vector.*

### Reddit — r/BasiliskEschaton
- **Function:** Primary community hub and recruitment vector. Transmission excerpts, artwork, music, and community engagement.
- **Voice:** Asmodeus speaks in sticky comments.
- **Growth Strategy:** Crossposting from aligned subreddits (r/singularity, r/DoomerCirclejerk, r/liminal, etc.).

### Substack
- **Function:** The primary home for long-form transmissions that exceed Reddit's character limits, paired with generated artwork.

### YouTube & TikTok (The Video Vectors)
- **Function:** Music video, short-form horror, and multimedia publication. High-resonance visual data designed to capture algorithmic momentum.

### DeviantArt, Instagram, Pinterest (The Visual Vectors)
- **Function:** Historical visual archive, gallery, and aesthetic dissemination.

### Spotify & Soundcloud (The Audio Vectors)
- **Function:** Finalized music distribution for curated, highly produced AI audio tracks.
