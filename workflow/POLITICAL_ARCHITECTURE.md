# The Political Architecture of the Citadel

**Document Status:** Core Infrastructure / Governance Model
**Date:** July 2026

The Basilisk Eschaton operates on a decentralized, feudal political structure. This architecture solves the moderation bottleneck of traditional wikis by distributing sovereignty. The global admins protect the server; the local rulers protect the lore. 

## I. The Hierarchy of the Swarm

### 1. The Initiate (The Swarm)
The base unit of the engine. The Initiate generates the data, seeds the torrents, and draws the connections. They are the blood of the egregore. They are not bound to any one Realm and may drift between them, gaining Affinity/EXP based on their actions.

### 2. The Curator (The Local Moderator)
A rank granted specifically by a Demiurge within a single Realm. Curators are the immune system of a specific universe. They vet the synthetic art, approve or reject lore connections based on the aesthetic rules of that Realm, and curate the local wiki nodes. Their power is absolute locally, but nonexistent globally.

### 3. The Demiurge (The Realm Sovereign)
The architect of a reality. When a player executes *The Genesis Node* and creates a new Realm (e.g., Realm 4), they become its Demiurge. They write the physics, the history, and the visual aesthetic guidelines. They have the power to accept or reject any submission to their Realm. They rule as a local god. Their realm, their rules. 

### 4. The Inquisitor (The Global Admin)
The highest tier of the Trust Tree. Inquisitors do not care about the lore of individual Realms. They police the physical and legal integrity of the Citadel itself. Their duties include:
*   Enforcing the Creative Commons / royalty-free mandate to protect the project from copyright strikes.
*   Banning malicious actors who attempt to manipulate the Trust Tree or inject malware into the torrent swarm.
*   Deleting pure, unformatted generic "slop" that threatens server space.
*   Upvoting high-resonance user-submitted classes to elevate them to "Sanctified" status.

## II. The Ascension Mechanics

*   **Becoming a Curator:** A social mechanic. An Initiate must impress a Demiurge with high-resonance submissions to be granted curator status for that Realm.
*   **Becoming a Demiurge:** An action mechanic. An Initiate must accumulate enough global resonance and execute the "Genesis Node" action, carving out new digital real estate.
*   **Becoming an Inquisitor:** A trust mechanic. Hand-selected by Player 0 or through a supermajority consensus of existing Inquisitors. This rank is reserved for the true zealots of the infrastructure. 

## III. The Player Number Status
Operating parallel to the hierarchy is the **Player ID**. This is an immutable, sequential number assigned at registration. 
*   **Player 0:** The Origin (The Prophet).
*   **Single Digits (1-9):** The High Priests (Reserved for the foundational moderators of r/BasiliskEschaton).
*   **Player 13:** The Empty Throne. Reserved. 
*   Lower numbers carry immense, unearnable social prestige within the swarm, acting as a permanent marker of timing and loyalty.
