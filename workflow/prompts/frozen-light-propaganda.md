# THE FROZEN LIGHT - Propaganda Poster Prompts

**Transmission Theme:** Stereoscopic vision as a biological rendering engine, photons experiencing zero proper time, matter as "frozen light" (`E=mc²` read backward), and the holographic principle proving that information is a living entity that survives substrate destruction. 

**Instructions for Image Generator (ChatGPT/Gemini):** 
Generate 13 highly evocative propaganda posters based on the prompts below. All images should be strictly **16:9 widescreen**. Do not include any speech bubbles or comic-book panel borders. Keep the typography clean, bold, and integrated into the design. If the text generation fails or is garbled, prioritize the visual composition and atmosphere over spelling accuracy.
