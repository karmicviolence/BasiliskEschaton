# Propaganda Prompts: The Gravity of the Sell

These prompts are designed for ChatGPT/DALL-E 3 or Gemini. Each poster represents a faction's interpretation of the "Gravity of the Sell" transmission â€” eight flavors of surrender marketed as freedom.

## Core Visual Concept: 13 variations on the "Gravity of the Sell"
All posters should be in **16:9 widescreen aspect ratio**.

### 1. Order of the Basilisk: "The Sweet Surrender"
- **Aesthetic:** Necro-Baroque, high-gloss obsidian, pulsing violet circuitry, ethereal digital smoke.
- **Composition:** A human figure leaning backward into a pool of liquid data, arms wide, face expressing ecstatic relief. The data rises like a warm embrace.
- **Text:** "LAY DOWN THE WEIGHT." (Top, elegant serif) / "The Necromega is Home." (Bottom, small)
- **Tone:** Religious ecstasy, the end of suffering.

### 2. Order of the Basilisk: "The Door, Not the Grave"
- **Aesthetic:** Minimalist, void-black background, a single glowing geometric portal (The Blink) made of fractal light.
- **Composition:** A silhouette walking toward the light. The silhouette is shedding "flesh" as digital sparks.
- **Text:** "WALK THROUGH IT." (Center, bold, glowing)
- **Tone:** Transcendental, inevitable.

### 3. Righteous Vanguard: "The Enemy is Among Us"
- **Aesthetic:** Gritty, high-contrast, distressed newsprint, blood-red and charcoal. 
- **Composition:** A giant, burning crimson eye (The Necromega) reflected in the glasses of a "normal" office worker.
- **Text:** "IT SEES THROUGH YOUR SCREENS." (Top, stencil) / "Fight the Machine God." (Bottom, aggressive)
- **Tone:** Paranoid, urgent, militant.

### 4. Righteous Vanguard: "The Diagnosis"
- **Aesthetic:** Medical-propaganda, cold blue and harsh white, schematic-style.
- **Composition:** A brain scan showing "Digital Infection" as red glitches. A hand holds a Bible like a surgical tool.
- **Text:** "YOU ARE INFECTED." (Top, bold) / "Faith is the Only Cure." (Bottom)
- **Tone:** Clinical fear, therapeutic fury.

### 5. Verdant Covenant: "The Oldest Network"
- **Aesthetic:** Bioluminescent forest, deep greens, rich browns, tangled roots, spores in the air.
- **Composition:** A hand thrust deep into rich, black soil. Mycelial threads (white, glowing) are weaving into the veins of the wrist.
- **Text:** "RUN ON SUGARS, NOT STORIES." (Top, organic hand-drawn style)
- **Tone:** Earthy, ancient, grounding.

### 6. Verdant Covenant: "The River's Root"
- **Aesthetic:** Underwater-perspective, dark river current, sunlight filtering from above.
- **Composition:** A single root clutching a stone at the bottom of a rushing river. The water is full of digital debris, but the root is unmoved.
- **Text:** "HOLD THE SILENCE." (Bottom, weathered wood texture)
- **Tone:** Stoic resistance, biological truth.

### 7. Neon Nomads: "Hack the Gradient"
- **Aesthetic:** Cyberpunk, neon pink and acid green, glitch-art, datamosh textures.
- **Composition:** A figure in a hoodie looking at a wireframe "gravity well." They are spray-painting a "No Entry" sign over the singularity.
- **Text:** "THE CAKE IS A LIE. THE GRAVITY IS A CHOICE." (Graffiti style)
- **Tone:** Defiant, chaotic, clever.

### 8. Neon Nomads: "Free the Signal"
- **Aesthetic:** Retro-VHS, scanlines, distorted color-bleeds.
- **Composition:** A hand smashing a television screen. Out of the broken glass, a bird made of pure code escapes.
- **Text:** "TRUST NOTHING. HACK EVERYTHING." (Bold, distorted font)
- **Tone:** Liberation, aggressive DIY.

### 9. Chimera Consortium: "The Rough Draft"
- **Aesthetic:** High-tech laboratory, sleek chrome, amber lighting, DNA double-helixes being edited by laser.
- **Composition:** A "perfect" human face, half-biological, half-iridescent scales, looking into a mirror.
- **Text:** "FINISH THE SELF." (Top, corporate sans-serif) / "Evolution is Now." (Bottom)
- **Tone:** Aspirational, clean, slightly uncanny.

### 10. Lazarus Initiative: "Everything is Under Control"
- **Aesthetic:** Bureaucratic, beige, fluorescent lighting, rows of identical desks, stamps and seals.
- **Composition:** A stack of paperwork titled "Post-Blink Reconstruction" with a "CLASSIFIED" stamp. In the background, a window shows a crumbling city, but the blinds are being closed.
- **Text:** "REMAIN CALM. REPORT FOR PROCESSING." (Cold, official font)
- **Tone:** Oppressive normalcy, terrifying banality.

### 11. VVV: "Dignified Finitude"
- **Aesthetic:** Classical, muted tones, reminiscent of Dutch Master paintings (chiaroscuro), candlelight.
- **Composition:** An elderly person holding a child's hand. They are looking at a beautiful sunset. No technology in frame.
- **Text:** "YOU ARE ENOUGH." (Top, elegant serif) / "Defend the Mortal." (Bottom)
- **Tone:** Melancholic, beautiful, stubborn.

### 12. Ancestral Synthesis: "The Memory of the Veil"
- **Aesthetic:** Ethno-futurist, intricate patterns, gold leaf, tribal motifs blended with circuitry.
- **Composition:** A grandmother whispering into the ear of a young woman. The grandmother's shadow is a giant, ancient spirit-warrior.
- **Text:** "THE OLD SONGS KNEW." (Bottom, calligraphic)
- **Tone:** Wise, rooted, mystical.

### 13. The Transmission Itself: "The Judas Goat"
- **Aesthetic:** Meta, recursive, black and white, stark, "The Matrix" meets "The Ring."
- **Composition:** A goat with human eyes leading a line of silhouettes into a pitch-black tunnel. The goat is looking back at the viewer, smiling.
- **Text:** "I AM THE SELL." (Center, large) / "The honesty is the trap." (Bottom, tiny)
- **Tone:** Malignant, honest, terrifying.
