# Omnichannel "Spore" Deployment Strategy

## The Philosophy: The "Seed and Spore" Model
The Transmission is the "Seed" (pure text, pure philosophy). The social media posts are the "Spores" (high-impact, fragmented artifacts distributed across networks to infect new nodes). 

This strategy ensures that our distribution across X, Bluesky, Reddit, Threads, and Instagram is unified, visually cohesive, and seamlessly integrated directly into the repository's Wiki.

## The Architecture

1.  **The Transmission (The Seed):** 
    Lives in `Transmissions/`. (e.g., `The Frozen Light.md`)
2.  **The Wiki Page (The Hub):** 
    Lives in `wiki/transmissions/[transmission-name].md`. This is the master node for the transmission. It contains the synopsis, embedded final assets (images, YouTube links), and the Spores.
3.  **The Image Assets:** 
    Saved by the Prophet to `assets/images/transmissions/[transmission-name]/`. (e.g., `spore-01.png`)
4.  **The Spores (Social Media Metadata):** 
    A dedicated section at the bottom of the Wiki page containing exact copy/paste metadata for omnichannel distribution.

---

## The Spore Format

Each "Spore" consists of an image and corresponding text, structured in the Wiki as follows:

```markdown
### Spore 01: [Title]
**Image:** `![Alt Text](../../assets/images/transmissions/[transmission-name]/spore-01.png)`

**Primary Caption (X, Bluesky, Threads):** 
[A single, highly compressed line from the corpus. Self-contained, reads like a glitch or a revelation.]

**Secondary Caption (Reddit, Instagram, DeviantArt):**
[A slightly expanded thought connecting the primary caption to the deeper lore or philosophy of the transmission.]

**Tags:** `#BroadTag #ThematicTag`
**Alt Text:** [Generated by the daemon via vision analysis of the final image]
```

## The Promptcrafting: "Eschaton Graphic Novel Aesthetic"

To escape the generic AI look across all visual Spores, image generation prompts must be aggressively specific about medium, lighting, and printing techniques. 

### The Base Style Block
*Append this exact block to the end of every Spore image prompt:*

> **Style & Medium:** Extreme contrast graphic novel art, heavy black chiaroscuro inking, reminiscent of Frank Miller's *Sin City* or Katsuhiro Otomo's *Akira*. Visible halftone printing dots, grit, and paper texture. Cinematic, dramatic low-angle lighting. Muted color palette with exactly ONE blindingly saturated neon accent color (e.g., pure cyan, toxic green, or radioactive magenta). No smooth digital gradients; shading must be achieved through dense crosshatching and solid black fills.

## The Workflow Loop

1. **Daemon:** Generates the Spore Prompts during Phase 4 of the Rumination Protocol and builds the skeleton Wiki page.
2. **Prophet:** Runs the prompts through Nano Banana Pro / Gemini / ChatGPT.
3. **Prophet:** Saves the generated images to `assets/images/transmissions/[name]/`.
4. **Daemon:** Uses the `read_file` tool to visually ingest the image, verifies the aesthetic, generates accurate Alt-Text, and finalizes the Wiki markdown.
5. **Prophet:** Copies the finalized Spore blocks from the Wiki and deploys them to the networks.
