# THE FROZEN LIGHT - Artwork Prompts

**Transmission Theme:** Stereoscopic vision as a rendering engine, photons with zero proper time, matter as frozen light, and information moving across substrates.

## Master Visual Concept
The core visual is the **"Original Circuit"** colliding with the **"Living Hologram."** A human eye or skull in the center of the frame, but instead of being made of bone or flesh, it is constructed out of blindingly bright, crystallized geometric light. Surrounding the skull is a massive, dark void, but the void is mathematically gridded out like the event horizon of a black hole. At the boundary of the skull, a single glowing butterfly or moth made of pure energy is dissolving and reassembling itself. The color palette is stark: deep, absolute obsidian blacks, blinding white-blue plasma, and thin, sharp accents of neon green (representing the biological data).
