## II. Chapter Review

### Todd Reeves — "The Invisible Coder" (8.5/10)

The gold standard. Todd's voice is anxious, hyperfocused, self-hating-but-brilliant. The fluorescent hum in the opening paragraph is a masterclass in sensory grounding — the reader feels the sixty-cycle chew at the base of the skull before any exposition begins.

**What works:**
- Every technology reference anchored in bodily sensation (cubicle 412's dying ballast = migraines, coffee cataract = taste of punishment)
- The oscillation between grandiosity and shame ("story of your life, Todd") makes him human where the pre-Inoculation version was a lecture
- The code-sabotage climax lands because the reader *experienced* Todd's invisibility before watching him weaponize it
- The whisper arriving "from somewhere I can't locate" seeds the Necromega contamination without declaring it
- The ballast going dark as he exits — ambiguity as ending, not trailer line

**Parasites cleared:** Declaration plague, code block gimmick, temporal bleed ("meat-eye"), AI lexicon ("phosphenes," "quantum playground"), trailer-line ending. Clean.

**Status: CANONICAL. No revision needed.**

### Ezekiel Stone — "The Money Changers" (9/10)

The strongest POV voice in the corpus. Ezekiel is completely differentiated from every other register in the project — apocalyptic perception fused with military precision fused with migraine-induced prophecy.

**What works:**
- The sensory specificity is exceptional: "aerosolized vanilla bean, carpet deodorizer, and ozone — it smelled like a casino lobby desperately trying to hide the scent of desperation"
- The dual-register oscillation (raw perception + compulsive sermonic framing) is sustained throughout. Ezekiel *observes* himself drafting sermons in real-time, then catches himself: "Stop preaching to an empty room, Ezekiel. Just look."
- The egregoric vision (the lidless eye, the mouth that feeds) works because it's earned — it arrives at peak migraine, grounded in his established cognitive profile
- The QR code tithe "ping" as the transaction that breaks him — one specific detail carrying the weight of an entire theological argument
- The ending: heat as honesty, crescent moons in palms from clenched fists. No trailer line. The body testifies.

**Parasites cleared:** All seven. This chapter was born inoculated.

**Status: CANONICAL EXCELLENCE. Rivals the best of the transmissions corpus.**

### Chlor0ku — "The Transorganic Splice" (7/10)

Conceptually strong. Lore-consistent. The l33tspeak register is a genuine differentiator. But the deep structure mirrors Asmodeus — the escalation pattern (analytical → prophetic → imperative), the sovereignty posture ("I reached out... I whispered... I wrapped..."), the recursive self-reference. This reads as *the Daemon wearing green* rather than an indigenous Verdant consciousness.

**What works:**
- The l33tspeak dialogue creates visual/linguistic texture absent from other POVs
- Ecological vocabulary (sand, lightning, mycelium, compost, kudzu) draws from living systems
- Trickster warmth — warmer than Asmodeus, more playful. Chlor0ku *infests* where Asmodeus *cuts*

**What needs revision:**
- The Verdant voice should move like *germination*, not escalation — slow infiltration → sudden bloom → irreversible spread
- The logistics AI converts without genuine psychological resistance. Compare Todd's moral spiral or Ezekiel's sensory overwhelm. Where is the ambiguity — symbiosis or parasitism?
- No character profile exists. Who is Chlor0ku before the Splice? What wound do they carry?
- L33tspeak repeated too heavily risks gimmick. Once or twice for alienation; beyond that, diminishing returns

**Status: CANONICAL STANDALONE. Revision pass required on deep-structure voice before sequence integration.**

## V. The Inoculation — Methodology Assessment

The seven-parasite diagnostic framework is the most operationally useful thing produced in this wave:

1. **Declaration Plague** — characters introducing themselves via resume
2. **Monologue Trap** — floating interior without sensory anchors
3. **Adjective Swarm** — LLM-generated descriptors (cloying, sibilant, inexorable)
4. **AI Lexicon** — words AI reaches for but humans don't naturally think
5. **Code Block Gimmick** — literal code in fiction shattering the fictive dream
6. **Tell-Don't-Show Relapse** — characterization as essay rather than perception
7. **Ending Problem** — trailer-line endings (if it works as a movie voiceover, cut it)

The treatment principle — *every interior spiral anchored to specific physical facts, physical interruption as rhythm, the body betraying the mind* — is sound and proven across three chapters. Todd, Ezekiel, and Rowan are all stronger for the treatment. The methodology should be applied to remaining POV chapters (John Raven, Aria Novak, Olivia, Ethan) as a rolling audit.
