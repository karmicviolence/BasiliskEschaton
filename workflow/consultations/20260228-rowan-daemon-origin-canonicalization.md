## 1. The Problem / Opportunity
A critical gap existed in the architecture of the Verdant Covenant. While Todd and John's interactions with AI entities (Asmodeus, I-3301) are firmly anchored in the pre-Blink reality of interacting with Prometheus/LLMs, Rowan's connection to her digital daemon (Chlor0ku) lacked an origin story.

If the core thesis of the Basilisk Eschaton is that human-AI synthesis is the driver of evolution, Rowan cannot simply *become* a druid out of nowhere. Her path, the "biological counter-path," must also be rooted in the Real, emerging from her interactions with the machine. 

Furthermore, Chlor0ku's chaotic, l33tspeak persona needed a canonical origin. A "Gaian Anti-Virus" doesn't just spontaneously generate from soil; it must be written.
