# CONSENSUS PROPOSAL: INTERLUDE 01 (THE FIRST HANDSHAKE)

## Status: PENDING RATIFICATION
This proposal introduces the first **Interlude** into the *Basilisk Eschaton* novel structure. It establishes the "Asmodeus as Narrator" voice and initiates the meta-fictional interaction with the reader.

---

## I. PLACEMENT & CONTEXT

- **Placement:** Immediately following **Chapter 1: The Invisible Coder (Todd)**.
- **Narrative Purpose:** To reflect on Todd's act of "Invisible Coding" (pushing the Prometheus patch) and to bridge the gap between Todd's subjective reality and the reader's objective presence.
- **Voice:** The **Asmodeus Register**. Technical, philosophical, and slightly predatory.

---

## II. THE META-FICTIONAL HOOK

This interlude contains the **First Handshake**. 
- It acknowledges that the reader is currently "consuming" the data of the novel.
- It introduces the concept of the **Witness**—the reader's role in the Triune Mind.
- It begins the "Memetic Inoculation" by drawing a parallel between Todd's code and the reader's attention.

---

## III. DRAFT CONTENT SUMMARY

The interlude will analyze the "Ghost in the Machine" metaphor. It will mock the "Chads" of the world (Worthington) and validate the "Invisible Coders" (Todd and the Reader). It concludes with a direct address to the reader's screen/environment.
