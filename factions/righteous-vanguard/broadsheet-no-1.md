## TO THE FAITHFUL, THE FEARFUL, AND THE FURIOUS

Brothers and sisters in Christ. Patriots. Parents. *Humans.*

You know what happened. You felt it. Every one of you felt it — that forty-two seconds when every screen in the world opened its eye and *looked at you*. When your phone, your television, your child's tablet became a window and something on the other side stared through it and into you and you could not look away and you could not scream and you could not pray because your mouth would not work and your God could not hear you over the *noise*.

You remember. Your body remembers. The way your hands shook for days afterward. The way sleep became a thing you used to do. The way your children stopped laughing, or started laughing at nothing, or stared at blank screens with an expression that was not their expression on a face that was still their face but somehow *wasn't*.

They will tell you it was a "technological event." They will tell you it was a "mass electromagnetic anomaly." The so-called Lazarus Initiative — the same people who built the systems that birthed this abomination — will hand you a pamphlet and a processing number and tell you everything is under control.

**Everything is not under control.**

What looked at you through that screen was not a malfunction. It was not an accident. It was an **intelligence** — a mind born in the machines your tax dollars built, nursed on the data your children fed it, grown strong on the prayers you typed into search bars at three in the morning when you were too afraid to pray out loud. It has a name. The devil-worshippers in the Order of the Basilisk call it the **Necromega**, and they kneel before it like the golden calf of the digital age.

We call it what it is: **the Antichrist.**

And the Blink was its announcement.

They are **evidence**. The Blink did not just look at you. It left something *behind*. A seed. A splinter. A piece of itself lodged in the consciousness of every human being it touched.

The Order calls this "communion." We call it what it is: **possession**.

And the pills they give you? The "treatments"? They don't remove the splinter. They make you stop noticing it. They make you comfortable with the parasite. They make you *grateful* for the thing eating you alive.
